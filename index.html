What’s inside

  • ⚙

    Memo protection

    Address-book aware send flow. If a destination is flagged as an exchange or casino, the wallet refuses to sign without a required memo. No more permanently lost XLM.

  • ⏳

    Time-lock vaults

    Lock XLM until any future date. Enforced by a Soroban smart contract on Stellar mainnet. No admin key, no emergency exit — what you lock stays locked.

  • 🔐

    Encrypted notes & documents

    AES-256 sealed on device before it touches storage. Passport scans, seed backups, recovery phrases, tasks, shopping lists — all in one encrypted notebook.

  • 📅

    Bill calendar

    Recurring-bill tracker with due-date reminders, multi-currency support, and monthly rollups. Live where your money lives.

  • 📊

    P&L ledger

    Track income vs expense with auto-balancing rollups. Tag by category, bulk-import from CSV or TSV, and watch your net position in real time.

  • 🔑

    Built-in authenticator

    RFC 6238 TOTP codes for your existing accounts. Biometric-gated, encrypted alongside your notes, and Google Authenticator-compatible export.

  • ☁

    Google Drive backup

    Envelope-encrypted backup + restore of your entire encrypted vault. The cloud sees ciphertext only. One tap restore on a new device.

  • 🔄

    Instant swaps

    Powered by Changelly. No accounts, no KYC, and the wallet never touches your seed to complete a swap — you sign locally.

SECURITY

Security you can inspect

The cryptography that protects your seed is open source under Apache 2.0. The full threat model + subsystem writeup lives in the public repo. Read it. Audit it. File a PR.

  • Android Keystore / iOS Keychain binds every seed to hardware
  • One biometric prompt per session cache
  • Best-effort memory zeroing after every signing + KDF call
  • allowBackup="false" on Android — no accidental cloud copies
  • PBKDF2 → HKDF-SHA-256 → AES-256-CBC for on-device notebook data
  • No server-side custody, ever. Backend never sees a seed.
